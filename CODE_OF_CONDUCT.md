# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in the ComfyFlow Insight community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

We are committed to creating an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

---

# Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

* Being respectful and professional
* Providing constructive feedback
* Accepting feedback gracefully
* Helping new contributors
* Focusing on improving the project
* Showing empathy toward community members

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

* Harassment or discrimination
* Trolling or insulting comments
* Personal attacks
* Publishing private information without permission
* Any conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional environment

---

# Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces, including:

* GitHub Issues
* Pull Requests
* Discussions
* Community Channels
* Documentation Contributions

It also applies when an individual is officially representing the project in public spaces.

---

# Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing community standards.

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, documentation edits, or other contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.

---

# Enforcement Guidelines

## Correction

Community Impact:

Use of inappropriate language or unprofessional behavior.

Consequence:

A private warning from project maintainers.

---

## Warning

Community Impact:

Repeated inappropriate behavior.

Consequence:

A warning with a request to stop the behavior.

---

## Temporary Ban

Community Impact:

Serious violations of community standards.

Consequence:

Temporary restriction from project participation.

---

## Permanent Ban

Community Impact:

Sustained harmful behavior or severe violations.

Consequence:

Permanent removal from project participation.

---

# Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it to the project maintainers through GitHub Issues or direct contact methods provided by the project.

All reports will be reviewed and investigated fairly and respectfully.
